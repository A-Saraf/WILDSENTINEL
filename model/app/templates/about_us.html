About Us

WildSentinel is an intelligent platform that uses ecological data, machine learning, and geospatial analysis to predict zoonotic spillover risks. By bringing together wildlife trade patterns, habitat loss trends, and disease history, we provide an early-warning system that helps protect both human health and biodiversity. Our goal is to make science-driven insights accessible, actionable, and impactful.

Key Features

  • 🌐 Data Engine: Combines wildlife trade, habitat loss, species distribution, and outbreak datasets.
  • 🤖 AI-Driven Risk Modeling: Predicts zoonotic spillover probabilities using advanced machine-learning algorithms.
  • 🗺️ Geospatial Hotspot Mapping: Visualizes high-risk zones through interactive heatmaps and GIS layers.
  • 🔄 Scenario Simulation: Tests how interventions—like reducing trade or restoring habitats—can lower spillover risk.
  • 📊 Insightful Dashboard: Presents analytics, charts, and maps in a clean, user-friendly interface.

Our Vision

We envision a world where technology strengthens the harmony between humans and nature. WildSentinel aims to empower communities and decision-makers with predictive insights that prevent outbreaks, preserve ecosystems, and promote a safer, healthier future for all.
